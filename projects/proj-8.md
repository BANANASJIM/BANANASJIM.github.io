## Build Smart, Fight Harder

**Scrap Frontier** is a physics-based strategy game developed for Brackeys Game Jam 2025.1, exploring the theme "Nothing can go wrong" with a humorous twist on component-based combat.

[![Play on itch.io](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzIwMTY5NDI2LnBuZw==/original/XKhXBV.png)](https://bananasjim.itch.io/scrap-frontier)

### Gameplay

Strategically assemble components to defeat enemies in this physics-driven puzzle game. Parts stick together upon contact but remain draggable for reconfiguration during battle. Players can also directly manipulate parts as weapons, embracing the game jam's theme that mishaps are part of the fun.

### Features

- **Physics-Based Assembly**: Dynamic part attachment system with real-time reconfiguration
- **Strategic Combat**: Build and adapt your contraption to overcome different challenges
- **Retro Aesthetics**: Pixel art style with sci-fi theme
- **Browser Playable**: HTML5 build available on itch.io

### Controls

- **A/D or Arrow Keys**: Navigate camera
- **Left-Click & Drag**: Interact with and position parts
- **R**: Restart level
- **M**: Toggle music
- **P**: Toggle sound effects

### Development

- **Engine**: Godot
- **Language**: GDScript
- **Development Time**: Game Jam (Brackeys 2025.1)
- **Genre**: Strategy, Physics, Puzzle
